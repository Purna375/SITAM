    • Our Mission

    Satya Institute of Technology and Management, Vizianagaram is a private Engineering College established in 2008. The institute is affiliated to JNTU, Kakinada and offers various courses in the field of Engineering & Technology and Management at the UG and PG level. Admission to the courses offered by SITAM are based on the candidate’s performance in various national and state level entrance exams such as AP EAMCET and AP PGECET. Additionally, SITAM, Vizianagaram has a group of 97 faculty members, who are highly qualified and experienced. SITAM also has a dedicated Training and Placement cell that provides placement assistance to the students. Some of the top recruiters are Symphony, TATA Steel and Quess Corp.

    To evolve into and sustain as a Centre of Excellence in Technological Education and Research with a holistic approach.

    To produce high quality engineering graduates with the requisite theoretical and practical knowledge and social awareness to be able to contribute effectively to the progress of the society through their chosen field of endeavor.
